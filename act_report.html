Relationship between retweets and likes of WeRateDogs tweets.¶

To show the relationship between number of retweets and likes, a linear regression plot was used.

Regression plot shows the relationship between a single dependent or response variable and one or more independent or predictor variables. Regression plot creates a regreesion line between 2 (two) parameters and helps to visualize their linear relationships.

Observation(s)¶
  1. There is an outlier in the plot.
  2. The chart shows a positive correlation between Retweets and Likes of WeRateDogs Tweets.
  3. The higher the number of retweets, the higher the number of likes.
